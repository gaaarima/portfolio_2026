Teesa is a luxury jewelry brand I founded to bridge two parts of my life: years working inside the fine-jewelry industry, and my Nepali heritage. Where most luxury brands borrow a generic European visual language, Teesa draws its identity from Newari textile patterns and traditional craftsmanship — a point of view no one else is offering.
