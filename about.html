About

A designer who
also builds.

I'm a UX and digital designer who believes the best products come from blending empathy with the right technology. I design integrated experiences that simplify complex workflows and keep people motivated — and because I can build the front end myself, I care about how a design actually ships, not just how it looks in Figma.

My path is a little different. Before design, I spent years in fine jewelry and restaurant management, so I think about products the way the people using them all day do. That same eye shows up in my creative work — illustration, nature photography, and the luxury jewelry brand I'm building, Teesa, which draws on my Nepali heritage and Newari craftsmanship.

I'm currently completing a Bachelor's in Web & Digital Design (4.0 GPA) and hold the Google UX Design Professional Certificate. My recent work spans inventory and fitness products that turn scattered tasks into clear, guided flows, plus live e-commerce design for a luxury jewelry storefront.
