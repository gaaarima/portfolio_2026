Sethi Fine Jewelry

E-commerce design and craft for a live luxury jewelry storefront, shopsethi.com.

Role

Sales Associate & Marketing Assistant contributing hands-on design and front-end work to the Sethi Fine Jewelry Shopify storefront.

What I worked on

  • Product image consistency — built a 2048×2048 Canva canvas system to standardize subject scale and anchor alignment, so every product photo sits consistently in the collection grid.
  • Collection grid — wrote custom Liquid code to fix image-ratio and scale inconsistencies across the grid.
  • Structured data — set up Shopify metafields for cleaner, structured product information.
  • Product descriptions — wrote and formatted polished copy for fine-jewelry designers including Moritz Glik and Spinelli Kilcollin.
  • Content — edited and formatted a "How to Layer Gold Jewelry" blog post, including hyperlinking featured products.

Why it matters

This is real e-commerce UX under real brand and merchandising constraints — the discipline of making a luxury catalog feel consistent, premium, and easy to shop.
